🌍 Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard

An interactive Business Intelligence dashboard analyzing global listing trends, pricing patterns, customer reviews, and host trust signals for Airbnb.

This project focuses on delivering actionable insights using real-world analytics storytelling techniques to understand market growth, customer behavior, and city-level performance.

🚀 Project Objective

The main objective of this dashboard is to:

Analyze listing growth lifecycle

Identify top performing cities

Understand review frequency & seasonality trends

Evaluate customer satisfaction metrics

Study host trust verification signals

This helps stakeholders make better decisions related to pricing strategy, market expansion, and customer experience optimization.

📌 Key Insights
📈 Listings Lifecycle Analysis

Listings experienced rapid growth from 2011–2015, reaching peak expansion.

Regulatory tightening slowed growth in 2016–17.

Recovery phase observed before sharp decline during COVID-19 impact period.

🌎 Market Share by City

Paris, New York, and Sydney contribute nearly 50% of total listings and reviews.

Hotel rooms show significantly higher pricing compared to other property types.

⭐ Customer Ratings Insights

Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro are the highest-rated cities overall.

Cleanliness and value-for-money are the most common areas needing improvement.

Hong Kong and Istanbul show comparatively lower satisfaction scores.

🔁 Review Behaviour

98.8% of customers review only 3 times or fewer, indicating low repeat review engagement.

A rare outlier customer posted 283 reviews, suggesting potential data anomaly.

📅 Seasonality Pattern

European cities dominate review share during summer travel months (April–August).

New York sees review spikes during holiday season (Nov–Dec).

🛡️ Host Trust Signals

Over 66% of hosts are fully verified, ensuring platform reliability.

Anonymous and unverified host presence remains minimal.
